About us

Picture yourself dining under a canopy of majestic mahogany trees. The air is filled with the tantalizing aroma of Indian and Filipino cuisine. You can sip on refreshing cocktails or lassi as you enjoy your meal surrounded by lush greenery. If you're looking for a unique dining experience with bold flavors and a peaceful setting, this is the spot for you.
